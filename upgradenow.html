Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +
Yes, you can change your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at your next billing date.
Is there a limit on file size? +
Free accounts can share files up to 500MB. Pro accounts up to 10GB per file, and Business/Enterprise accounts up to 50GB per file. Contact us for custom solutions if you need to share larger files.
How secure is my data? +
All files are encrypted with AES-256 encryption, both in transit and at rest. We use zero-knowledge encryption for password-protected files, meaning even we cannot access your protected files.
What happens if I cancel my subscription? +
You can continue using the service until the end of your billing period. After cancellation, your account will revert to the Free plan and you may lose access to some files if you exceed the Free storage limit.
